Why?

Do what you can't. Achieve what you have not. And most certainly have fun. Go after it. Realise your vision. Dream now dream later, but don't stop. Stumble across the sand, catch your breath, wipe your sweat, dry your tears, and steel yourself. Awaken.








Why Not?

Well, consider: Could the world justifiably be called infinite? Doubtful. It may not have a discernable end, but it had a beginning. And its component parts certainly have a limited cosmological shelf life. Splitting hairs or not, if history tells us anything, it's that scientists often make poor poets. We're all just a ship of fools chasing phantoms, heedless as to what actually underwrites natural law.
